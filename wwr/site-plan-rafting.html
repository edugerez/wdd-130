Overview

Purpose

The main purpose of the "Rafting Adventure" site is to provide general information about the rafting service and generate pre-sales through the site.

The website is designed to attract customers, provide information, and generate pre-sales as well as analyze business growth by statistics of visits and sales through the site.

Audience

The website wants to reach both young and family audiences, seeking to provide a fun moment as part of anti-stress therapy.

At the same time, it seeks to capture an audience willing to pay competitive rates in order to guarantee a complete service.
The audience will access the site through desktop and mobile devices but not through an application.
Attempts are made to attract potential clients by focusing marketing on searches for fun, vacations, anti-stress therapies, and searches for places near the location of the service.
